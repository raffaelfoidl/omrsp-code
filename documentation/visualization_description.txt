* The x-axis shows the release years and names of films that were awarded an Oscar in the "Best Picture" category.
  For instance, a release year of 1994 typically means that the movie won the oscar at the ceremony in 1995.

* The first (left) y-axis associates the movies with the gross revenue they generated in the USA.
  This axis is represented by the green line.

* The second (right) y-axis juxtaposes the movies with their respective IMDb user score.
  This axis is represented by the blue line.
